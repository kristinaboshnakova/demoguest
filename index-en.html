About us

Welcome to Balmora Residence – suitable for those who appreciate comfort and convenience combined with first-class service – families, private groups, corporate events and guests looking for an unforgettable experience in the heart of the Balkan mountain.

Location

Balmora Residence is an elegant newly built villa, located at the foot of the majestic Botev Peak, in the picturesque “Panitsite” area near the town of Kalofer.

Find us →

Comfort

Rooms with comfortable beds, private bathrooms, terraces with a view, fast Wi-Fi and everything you need for a peaceful stay.

Learn more →

Amenities

The house offers: a spacious living room with a large table, soft seating and a modern fireplace, a large fully equipped kitchen, a veranda and a large balcony with a view towards the Balkan mountain and Botev Peak, a huge yard suitable for children’s games or outdoor events, enclosed parking, charcoal barbecue, board games and toys for children, bicycles for rides around the area.
